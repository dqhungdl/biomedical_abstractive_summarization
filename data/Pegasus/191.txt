Epidermolysis bullosa (EB) is a group of disorders in which skin blisters form after a minor injury. Other complications of Herlitz JEB can include fusion of the fingers and toes, abnormalities of the fingernails and toenails, joint deformities (contractures) that constrict movement, and hair loss (alopecia). Most affected individuals do not have extensive scarring or granulation tissue formation, so breathing difficulties and other severe complications are rare.
