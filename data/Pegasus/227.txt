At least 1 million people a year in the United States get shingles. It can also reduce pain in people who still get shingles after being vaccinated. The shingles vaccine is a safe and easy shot that may keep you from getting shingles. You should get the shot even if you have already had shingles or don't remember having chickenpox. In clinical trials, the shingles vaccine reduced the risk of shingles by 50%.
