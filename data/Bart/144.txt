Lactic acid is produced when oxygen levels, become low in cells. The most common cause of lactic acidosis is severe medical illness. Intense exercise or convulsions can cause temporary cause the condition. Certain diseases can also cause the Condition including: AIDS Alcoholism Cancer Cirrhosis Cyanide poisoning Kidney failure Respiratory failure Sepsis (severe infection) Some medicines can rarely cause lactic Acidosis: Certain inhalers used to treat asthma or COPD Epinephrine An antibiotic called linezolid Metformin, used to treats diabetes (most often when overdosed) One type of medicine used to Treat HIV infection Propofol. The main treatment for lacticacidosis is to correct the medical problem that causes it.
