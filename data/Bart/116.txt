Some tips that may help reduce swelling: Put your legs on pillows to raise them above your heart while lying down. Follow a low-salt diet, which may reduce fluid buildup and swelling. Wear support stockings (sold at most drugstores and medical supply stores) When traveling, take breaks often to stand up and move around. Avoid wearing tight clothing or garters around your thighs. Lose weight if you need to. Follow your health care provider's treatment recommendations. If you have long-term swelling, ask your provider about options to prevent skin breakdown, such as: - Flotation ring - Lamb's wool pad - Pressure-reducing mattress.
