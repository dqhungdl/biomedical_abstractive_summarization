Arthritis is inflammation of one or more joints. A joint is the area where two bones meet. Osteoarthritis usually comes with age and most often affects the fingers, knees, and hips. Reactive Arthritis is pain or swelling in a joint that is caused by an infection in your body. Rheumatoid arthritis happens when the bodys own defense system doesnt work properly. Gout is caused. by crystals that build up in the joints. It usually affects the big toe, but many other joints may be affected. Juvenile Ar arthritis is the term used to describe arthritis in children.
