A suprapubic catheter (tube) drains urine from your bladder. Insert a catheter into your urethra through your urinary opening in your labia (women) or penis (men) You will need to check your catheter and bag throughout the day. You will get a prescription to buy special catheters at a medical supply store. Drink plenty of water during the day to keep urine flowing. Call your health care provider if: - You are having trouble changing yourCatheter or emptying your bag. - Your bag is filling up quickly, and you have an increase in urine. - You have urinary retention, urinary retention (not being able to urinate), surgery that made the catheter necessary, or another health problem.
