Metoprolol may cause side effects. Tell your doctor if any of these symptoms are severe or do not go away. Know which other medications and foods you need to avoid. Ask your health care provider or pharmacist if you have questions. If you experience a serious side effect, you or your doctor may send a report to the Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) MedWatch Adverse Event Reporting program online (http://www.fda.gov/Safety/MedWatch) or by phone (1-800-332-1088) Click here for more information about the FDA's MedWatch program and how to report side effects of drugs you are taking or to get information on how to get help with a prescription or over-the-counter drug. For confidential support, call the Samaritans on 08457 90 90 90, visit a local Samaritans branch, or see www.samaritans.org for details. In the U.S., call the National Suicide Prevention Line on 1- 800-273-8255.
